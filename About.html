Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to facilitate a vision for LGBTQ+ youth.

It pulls together the stories of incredible LGBTQ+ athletes into one place.

Hopefully, this will help young people to find and learn about relatable mentors.

Feel free to submit people for inculsion in this site via the Submissions section.

Criteria to be satisfied:

In order for someone ot appear on the site they must satisfy the following three criteria:

1. Forever Pride

  • Is this person currently and in the past been progressive, positive outspoken in their support of LGBTQ values?


    • 2. Achiever

  • Is this person successful in their field / interest?

    • 3. Respectful of others

  • Is this person respectful of others?
